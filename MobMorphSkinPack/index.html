Mob Morph Skin Pack for Minecraft: Bedrock Edition>/h2>

The MobMorphSkinPack is a skin pack for Minecraft: Bedrock Edition that lets you morph into a variety of mobs!

To get the skin pack, you will need the following:

The skin pack itself
LOADINGBYPASS.mcpack
IObit Unlocker

Start by installing IObit Unlocker. Then download the skin pack and the LOADINGBYPASS.mcpack file. Now open IObit Unlocker and click the "Add" button. Now locate to this directory:

C:\Program Files\WindowsApps\Microsoft.MinecraftUWP\data\skin_packs

Now click the "persona" folder and press "OK". Now click the little arrow next to the "Unlock" button and click "Unlock and Delete". NNow close IObit Unlocker and open it again.

Once loaded into IObit Unlocker, click the "Add" button again but this time, locate to the skin pack. (Make sure you extracted the archive so that a folder called "persona" comes out of it.

Select the "persona" folder from the Mob Morph Skin Pack then click the arrow next to the "Unlock" button and click "Unlock and Copy" this time. Now copy it to the same directory. Incase you forgot, the directory is:

C:\Program Files\WindowsApps\Microsoft.MinecraftUWP\data\skin_packs

Now open Minecraft and double click the "LOADINGBYPASS.mcpack" file you downloaded earlier and now go to your skin packs section and enjoy the skins!
